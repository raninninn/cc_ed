ed
cc_ed is a line-oriented text editor. Its aim is to provide an experience on par with GNU ed.
To learn Ed, visit https://www.gnu.org/software/ed/manual/ed_manual.html.

To edit a file, enter `ed FILENAME`.

For feature requests and bug reports, visit https://github.com/raninninn/cc_ed.

Still don't know why you should give Ed a try? Visit https://www.gnu.org/fun/jokes/ed-msg.html to find out why Ed is the standard editor.
